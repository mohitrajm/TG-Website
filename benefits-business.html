A Practical and Cleaner Fuel Choice

Industries in India are looking for cleaner energy options that do not disrupt operations. Biomass briquettes make this shift possible. They burn cleaner than coal and are more consistent than firewood, which makes them suitable for many industrial heating applications.

Key Benefits for Industries

Cleaner fuel compared to coal

Briquettes release less sulfur, smoke, and ash while maintaining stable heat output.

Better for ESG and sustainability goals

Using renewable fuel supports your company’s sustainability reporting and environmental commitments.

Consistent form compared to firewood

Uniform briquettes are easier to store, handle, and feed into boilers.

Stable supply and reliable performance

We focus on consistent quality so you can run your operations smoothly.

Support for responsible sourcing

Your fuel choice helps reduce stubble burning and supports farmers.

Better for Business and Better for the Air

Turning crop residue into briquettes prevents open burning and reduces the use of fossil fuels.

Each ton of briquettes used can help:

  • Reduce carbon emissions
  • Replace a portion of coal or firewood
  • Lower local pollution levels
  • Support cleaner communities

Cleaner operations benefit both your facility and the surrounding environment.

Where Biomass Briquettes Can Be Used

Briquettes are suitable for a wide range of boilers and industrial processes, including:

  • Food processing
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Chemicals and manufacturing
  • Dairy and milk units
  • Brick kilns
  • Textiles
  • Hospitality and commercial heating

If you are not sure about compatibility, we can guide you based on your current system.
