Quality and Safety at the Core

We follow a structured process to maintain quality. As the facility expands, we plan to introduce more quality checks and automation. This includes:

  • Checking moisture levels of raw material
  • Ensuring consistent density and size in briquettes
  • Maintaining clean and safe work areas
  • Following safety guidelines for machinery
  • Tracking production performance

Designed for Future Growth

Our facility is planned with long-term growth in mind. We want this facility to become the base for multiple clean energy and waste-to-resource products. The same location can support:

  • Pellet production
  • Biochar production
  • Bio-bitumen experiments
  • R&D for cleaner materials and fuels
  • Additional briquette lines
