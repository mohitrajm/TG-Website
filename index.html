India Has a Waste Problem and an Energy Problem

Every year, India produces millions of tons of crop residue and other biomass waste. A lot of it is burned in the open or left unused. At the same time, many industries still depend heavily on coal and firewood for their energy needs.

This leads to:

  • More air pollution
  • Wasted resources
  • Higher health and environmental costs

We didn’t “discover a new gap.” Biomass is already a mature industry. But the opportunity is still huge. India produces so much waste that there is space for many responsible players to operate. We chose to start in this sector because it’s practical, proven, and badly needs scale with quality.

We Turn Waste Into Clean, Useful Fuel

Thryve Green takes what would have been waste and turns it into something valuable — biomass briquettes.

1. We Work With Farmers & FPOs

We source crop residue and other biomass from farmers, FPOs, and local suppliers, creating an extra income stream from material that usually gets burnt or thrown away.

2. We Process It Into Briquettes

Using the right machinery and process, we convert this biomass into dense, uniform briquettes that work as a cleaner replacement for coal and firewood.

3. We Supply It to Industries

We provide these briquettes to industries that want to reduce their emissions, improve ESG scores, and move toward cleaner energy without disrupting their operations.

Briquettes Are Just the Beginning

We’re starting with biomass briquettes because they solve a real problem today — they help reduce coal use and cut down pollution from open burning.

But we don’t see ourselves as “just a briquette company.”

Our mission is to turn India’s waste into clean energy, materials, and fuels that reduce pollution and make sustainability effortless.

In the future, this can include things like biomass-based fuels, bio-bitumen, and other products that replace fossil-based materials. Briquettes are simply our first step — our launchpad.

For Farmers and FPOs

We want farmers to benefit directly from this shift to clean energy.

  • Earn from crop residue: Turn leftover stalks, husk, and other waste into an income source instead of burning it.
  • Cleaner fields, less burning: Avoid stubble burning and prepare your fields faster and in a cleaner way.
  • Support cleaner air: Reduce smoke in your village and nearby areas by sending your waste to us instead of burning it.
  • Long-term partnership mindset: We aim to build stable, repeat relationships with farmers and FPOs, not one-time deals.

For Businesses and Industries

If you’re using coal or firewood today, biomass briquettes can be a cleaner, more responsible option.

  • Replace coal and firewood: Use briquettes as a clean-burning fuel in boilers, heaters, and industrial processes.
  • Reduce emissions: Lower local pollution and support your company’s decarbonization goals.
  • Support ESG goals: Use a fuel that aligns with your environmental and social commitments.
  • Stable, consistent fuel form: Uniform briquettes are easier to handle and store compared to loose firewood.
